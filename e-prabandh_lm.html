The World is a book and those who do not travel , read only a page

  • Tourism in India is economically important and is growing rapidly.Rajasthan is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India, for both domestic and international tourists. Rajasthan attracts tourists for its historical forts, palaces, art and culture. Every third foreign tourist visiting India also travels to Rajasthan as it is part of the Golden Triangle for tourists visiting India.


  • e Prabandh yojna is an idea through which we want to provide the tourist with all the possible necessities that a tourists require , at one place . That’s why the name“e-Prabandh Yojna “.This yojna has the following features :-


  • 1) GOVERNMENT HOTELS at affordable cost.There will be a facility of room sharign to increase the sense of togetherness and promote tourists interaction .


  • 2) TOURIST BUS from hotels to various tourists attractions at flexible time and it will develop a sense of togetherness among the tourists.


  • 3) Govt. Registered TOURIST GUIDE AND SECURITY GUARD for the safety of tourists from theft and fraud.


  • 4) Govt. FOOD STALLS and REVIEW CENTRES at the tourist spots for providing hygienic food and improve the tourist spot based on the reviews received at review centers


  • 5) MEDICLAL FACILITY / CLINIC at e-prabandh hotels and buses to provide tourists safe and healthy stay and travel.


  • 6) e-Prabandh card for Digitalizing the whole tourism process. 20 % off on on giving the review ,on the next stay.


  • 7) RATING AND REVIEW of hotel services and tourists spots so that the services and the tourist places can be improved on the various parameters .
