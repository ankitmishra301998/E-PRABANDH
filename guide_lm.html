The guides under E-PRABANDH are best because of the following reasons...

  • 1) The tourist guides for e- Prabandh buses will be government registered and well trained in all aspects.


  • 2) The guides will have accurate knowledge of the places which the tourists will be taken .


  • 3) The guides will have good communications skills and knowledge of several languages to handle the tourists from various places.


  • 4) The guides will have a friendly and an understandable nature.


  • 5) The security gaurds in the tourist buses will also be government registered .


  • 6) The gaurds will be well trained to protect the tourists from any kind of theft, fight etc at tourist places.
